# AI Chatbot Documentation

## 1. Features
The AI Customer Assistant helps clients understand the platform by answering **general, non-private questions** such as:
- how booking works
- what payment methods are accepted
- what services are available
- how cancellation and refund rules work
- what the assistant can and cannot do

However, strictly limited to answer general questions and provide what are provided rather than extensive use or taking action
on behave pf user(client)

## 2. Important Safety / Privacy Boundary
The assistant is intentionally limited.

It is allowed to use:
- general platform workflow information
- public service / offering information
- general policy information

It is not allowed to use directly:
- private booking details
- user payment history
- saved payment method details
-  user data
- direct database access

The AI receives context only from the backend service layer.
